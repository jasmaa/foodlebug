About

Got food? Don't want it? Post it on Foodlebug

Our Mission

Too many leftovers from catering or maybe just want to get rid of that old box of granola bars? Don't throw it away! Foodlebug provides an easy-to-use platform for sharing leftover food with the added benefit of being able to locate leftovers in your local area in hopes of reducing food waste locally.

How Do I Use Foodlebug?

Locating food is as simple as finding postings in your local area using our homepage map. You can also see a list of the most recent postings below. To share leftovers, create an account and post a picture and description of your food. Location can optionally be shared to make it easier for others to find your leftovers.
